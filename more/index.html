More about me


I’ve been studying acoustic guitar for years, mainly interested in country fingerstyle affected by fantastic Tommy Emmanuel. I’m maintaining a python module guitarHarmony inspired by some wonderful music books for learners practicing. I like to collect and play all kinds of wired cubes and has a lifetime fastest record 12.88s on Rubik’s cube. Billiard games are my favorite as well, no matter 8-ball, 9-ball and/or snooker. I do not have strong desire of winning any more but fortunately I’m always enjoying the playing and watching competitions. One of my best friend Wang once said

Nothing you can predict, make your best strategies and keep going, you know, the ball is round.

He may be correct though I don’t see what’s the connection between a round ball and prediction. Whatever, I do think balls are round is important.
