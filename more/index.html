More about me


I’ve been studying acoustic Guitar for years, mainly interested in country fingerstyle affected by fantastic Tommy Emmanuel five years back. Occasionally, I play and sing folk, popular or rock either when I’m excited or frustrated, while it’s hard for me to sing pleasantly anyways. I like all kinds of billiard games, including 8-ball, 9-ball and snooker, never have enough odds of ‘success’ but luckily I don’t care about winning and can always enjoy the playing time. One of my best opponent and friend Wang once said: Nothing you can predict, make your best strategies and keep going, you know, the ball is round. He may be correct though no clear logic on what’s the connection between round ball and prediction. Whatever, balls are round is important.
